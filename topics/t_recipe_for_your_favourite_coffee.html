Recipe for your favourite coffee

Example recipe for moka pot

Learn how to make coffee like from a coffee shop


Prepare the following ingredients:
  • 100 ml moka pot coffee
  • 100 ml milk
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
  • Toppings (optional)
Prepare the following equipment:
  • Moka pot
  • Milk steaming device (milk steamer or microwave/stovetop and a whisk)
  1. Prepare your moka pot coffee
  2. Heat your milk.
  3. Froth your milk.
  4. Add chocolate syrup.
  5. Add your moka pot coffee.
  6. Add the hot milk and foam.
  7. Add toppings and enjoy!
